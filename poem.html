❤️ KOTHA ❤️
Poem

My dearest Kotha,

My heart's true love,

Your radiant spirit shines from above,

Your smile, so bright, it warms my soul,

In your presence, I feel wholly whole.

Your kindness knows no bounds, it's true,

Your talents and passions, I deeply admire too,

Each moment with you is a precious gift,

Your very presence, my spirits does lift.

I long to hold you close, to feel your embrace,

To gaze into your eyes, and see your lovely face,

For you have captured my heart, body and mind,

In you, my soulmate, my love, I do find.

Please, my darling Kotha, say you'll be mine,

Let our lives intertwine, our futures align,

I promise to love you, to cherish you true,

For my heart beats only for you, Kotha, my love, it's you.
